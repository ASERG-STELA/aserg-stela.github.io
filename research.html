ASERG@SHU has a strong focus on applied software engineering research. It's members have a track record of industry and applied projects.

The group explores topics like: Software Development Process, Software Architecture, Business Processes, Service-orientation, Microservices and DevOps.

The group has experience with the development of applications in the domains of: Smart Cities, Cloud computing, Internet of Things, Digital Identity Management and Access Control.
