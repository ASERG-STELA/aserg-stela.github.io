The Applied Software Engineering Research Group aims to conduct research and development activities on software engineering with an interdisciplinary view of its application. The group congregates researchers and practitioners from different disciplines, rooted on strong software engineering foundations and its interplay with cyber security.

Under the leadership of Dr. Carlos da Silva we are part of the Department of Computing at Sheffield Hallam University. The group started in 2014 as the SmarT Environment LAboratory of the Metropole Digital Institute at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte, Brazil.

You can find more about the group in these pages.
